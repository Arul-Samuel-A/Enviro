Join hands for a Better Future!

Together, we can make a change!

For just $2, you can plant a tree and make a difference in the fight against climate change. Simply click on the "Plant Now" button, choose the number of trees you'd like to plant, and complete the donation securely.

Once your donation is processed, we’ll ensure your tree is planted in areas where reforestation is needed most.

Who we are?

Enviro, we are a passionate organization committed to making a real, measurable impact—one tree at a time. For just $2, you can join us in planting trees that restore ecosystems and contribute to a greener, more sustainable planet. Our mission is to empower individuals and communities to take meaningful action in protecting the environment.

With your support, we can reduce carbon footprints, foster biodiversity, and help create a healthier future for generations to come. Together, we can make a lasting difference, starting with a single tree.

Volunteer with Us

Join Our Mission!

At Enviro, we believe that every individual can make a difference. Volunteering with us is a great way to contribute to a sustainable future while connecting with your community.

You can help us with

  • Planting Events: Participate in hands-on activities to plant trees in local parks and communities.
  • Outreach: Help spread awareness about the importance of trees and environmental protection.
  • Fundraising: Assist in organizing fundraising events to support our mission.
  • Social Media & Marketing: Share our mission and events through your networks.

We welcome volunteers of all backgrounds and experience levels. Most volunteer opportunities require a commitment of a few hours per event.
